This workbook previews the nine core units of AP Chemistry — the concepts, landmark discoveries, key scientists, and AP-style questions that will define your year. Use it this summer to build the quantitative and conceptual foundation you need to think like a chemist on day one.

⚗️
What AP Chem is really about AP Chemistry is built around six Big Ideas: atomic structure, bonding and intermolecular forces, chemical reactions, kinetics, thermodynamics, and equilibrium. The exam is 60 multiple-choice questions plus 7 free-response questions — heavily emphasizing mathematical reasoning, experimental design, and applying concepts to novel situations. Memorizing facts is not enough; you must understand why.
